PAF-IAST

PAF-IAST is an initiative of the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa in collaboration with Austrian and Chinese Universities. Our aim is to raise the quality of technical education in Pakistan through skill training and human resource development.

Admissions

At PAF IAST, we are committed to promoting equal opportunities and welcoming students from all backgrounds, irrespective of their creed, color, gender, ethnicity, and their social and financial status. Pak Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology strongly encourages female students to join any program of their choice and realize their dreams.

With a focus on innovation and collaboration with international partners, PAF-IAST offers cutting-edge programs to prepare students for global challenges.

International

Explore, Learn, and Grow Beyond Borders

At PAF-IAST, we believe in the power of international collaboration. Our students thrive globally through exchange programs with esteemed partners in Austria, China, and Germany, while we warmly welcome students from across the world to join our vibrant campus. Expand your horizons, gain invaluable experiences, and shape your future with our international initiatives.

Technology Park

To boost innovation through the technology Park, every faculty member, trained in Austria and China, will be offered an average grant of USD 50,000.

Partnerships

Establishing mutually beneficial relations with premier international institutes for human resources, technology transfer and research collaboration.

Scholarships

PAF IAST offers different types of local and foreign scholarship opportunities on a need and merit basis. Financial Aid is also granted to deserving candidates.

Internships

Mandatory for every student, to do an internship, every semester in an appropriate industry for practical experience related to their respective degree programs.
